Property Sales Dashboard Power BI

A comprehensive real estate analytics dashboard built with Power BI, analyzing 160 property sales across 11 countries over 3 years (2022–2024).

Total Revenue 96M
Total Income 67M

Top Sales Channel Broker (41%)

Page 1 — Revenue & Sales Overview
- Revenue By Month 
July records peak revenue, revealing strong mid-year seasonality

- Revenue By Country 
Italy & Japan lead all markets
- Revenue By Channels 

Broker dominates at 41%, Direct channel offers highest profit margin

-Revenue By Payment Status
 60.4% pending collection signals urgent cash flow risk

Page 2 — Expense & Financial Analysis
- Total Amount by Expense Type
 Property Taxes (38.85%) & Maintenance (38.68%) represent 77% of all expenses

- Clients By Payment Type
 96 clients (60%) pending payment requires immediate credit policy review

Page 3 — Client & Property Insights
- Property Price by Occupation 
Lawyers are the highest-spending segment

- Property Amount & Revenue by Occupation 
Renovation shows highest cost-to-revenue efficiency

- Properties Sold by Year 
2023 was peak year; 2024 shows sharp decline requiring investigation

- Revenue By Country & Year
